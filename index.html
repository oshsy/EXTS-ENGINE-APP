🚀 CORE FEATURES

1. Performance Modes

  • Power Saving Mode: Prioritizes battery life and lower temperatures.
  • Daily Mode: Balanced settings for comfortable everyday use.
  • Medium Mode: Maintains stable and balanced performance.
  • Extreme Mode: Pushes the device beyond its normal limits for heavy workloads.

2. Smart System Management

  • Automatic Screen Resolution: Reduces CPU/GPU workload dynamically (especially in Extreme Mode).
  • Logic Bot Engine: Analyzes hardware conditions and automatically boosts performance for whitelisted apps.
  • Process Limiter & App Blocking: Restricts non-whitelisted apps and blocks blacklisted background tasks.
  • Sensor Shutdown Mode: Disables camera, microphone, gyroscope, and compass for enhanced privacy.

3. Display & Graphics Controls

  • Manual Controls: Adjust display resolution down to ultra-low settings and force custom refresh rates.
  • Synthetic Compositor: Improves frame smoothness and visual consistency using additional RAM.

4. Tweaks & Hardware Controls (Root Required)

  • Thermal Limit Removal: Removes thermal throttling restrictions.
  • Max Hardware Force: Forces CPU and GPU frequencies to remain at their highest available levels.

📋 REQUIREMENTS

  • Non-Root: Android 11.0+ with Shizuku support (recommended).
  • Root Features: Magisk / KernelSU / APatch with root access granted.
